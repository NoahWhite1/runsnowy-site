Privacy Policy for Run Snowy

Last updated: 12/19/2025

Run Snowy (“the App”) is developed by Noah White (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you play our mobile game.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect personal information directly. However, the App uses third-party SDKs that may collect data automatically for advertising and analytics purposes.

1. IronSource LevelPlay Mediation (Ads)

The App uses IronSource LevelPlay mediation to display ads. Ads may be provided by IronSource and other supported ad networks, including Unity Ads, Tapjoy, Google AdMob, and Meta / Audience Network. These providers may collect:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA on iOS)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location (city-level)
  • App usage events
  • Ad interactions (views, clicks)

This information helps show relevant ads, limit repeated ads, prevent fraud, and measure performance.

2. Unity Analytics (if enabled)

If Unity Analytics is enabled, it may collect gameplay events and device information.

How We Use the Information

  • Serve advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Improve game experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Third-party ad networks may use contextual ads only when required by law.

Third-Party Services

Our game uses the following external services:

  • IronSource (LevelPlay) Privacy Policy
  • Unity Technologies Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Meta / Audience Network Privacy Policy
  • InMobi Privacy Policy

Your Choices

You can reset or limit ad tracking from your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking
